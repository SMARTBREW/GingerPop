Comparing Numbers (Bada, Chhota, Ya Barabar!)

Psst! Do you know how to compare numbers? Yeh bilkul alligator (magarmach) ke muh ki tarah hai! Bada number dekhkar alligator apna muh us taraf khol leta hai! 🐊

Key Points

1Greater Than (>) matlab bada hai (e.g. 8 > 4) aur Less Than (<) matlab chhota hai (e.g. 2 < 3).
2Digits check karo: Jis number mein zyada digits hain, wo hamesha bada hoga! (e.g., 254 > 99).
3Decimal check: Pehle whole part compare karo (3.4 vs 3.09 -> 3.4 bada hai!).
4Hinglish Fun Rule: Magarmach hamesha bade (larger) number ko khana chahta hai!
